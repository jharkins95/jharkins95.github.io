Research Projects

Pressure-Sensitive Yoga Mat

Advisor: Dr. Rahul Mangharam, Professor, ESE

Partners: Kevin Volkel, Dr. Marco Beccani

This is a summer research project that I undertook in mLab, an embedded systems research lab at Penn.

The goal was to design a 12-by-12 inch fabric mat capable of reading the pressure exerted on individual elements and lighting electroluminescent panels according to the pressure applied.

I designed the system hardware architecture, which included selecting components and creating the PCB layouts for both the power supply and the sensor reading/amplification circuitry. I also implemented the software for reading the pressure data and controlling the lights' brightness.
